LeanOps

LeanOps is a data-driven continuous improvement initiative that combines Lean principles with operational analytics to eliminate waste, optimize workflows, and enhance productivity to drive measurable gains in OEE and resource utilization at Exide Industries Ltd, One of the largest Battery Manufacturer in Asia supplying over 60 countries across 6 continents.

Problem Identification

Business Challenge

Automated Packaging & Dispatch lines were under‚Äëperforming on Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE). Minor stoppages, sub‚Äëoptimal break alignment, and inconsistent response time were depressing availability and performance.

  • OEE baseline in the 70-72% range
  • High proportion of minor stops and speed loss
  • Manual KPI collation causing reporting lag

Diagnostic Analysis (DMAIC)

Define

  • Partnered with the Production Manager and Maintenance Head to scope a 90-day diagnostic on the Packaging & Dispatch line.
  • Identified chronic losses: ~15% downtime from minor stoppages and 8% speed loss, directly impacting line throughput.
  • Established a business goal to increase OEE, improve utilization without additional headcount, and sustain throughput.

Measure

  • Extracted 300+ production-cycle records PLC and line reports along with conducting time study for cross verifying data.
  • Designed a centralized data pipeline in Excel and automated Power BI dashboards for real-time visibility of OEE, downtime trends, and speed loss.
  • Cut report preparation time by 30%, freeing line supervisors to focus on problem-solving rather than manual data entry.

Analyze

  • Applied Pareto analysis, 5-Whys, and FMEA to prioritize top downtime drivers.
  • Discovered that maintenance delays, overlapping break schedules, and absence of real-time stoppage logging were causing the bulk of avoidable losses.
  • Mapped end-to-end material and information flow between Packaging and Dispatch to visualize bottlenecks and quantify their impact.

Improve

  • Collaborated with the maintenance team to implement a dedicated technical team for conveyor and robotic arms reducing MTTR (Mean Time to Repair) on critical stoppages.
  • Proposed a re-sequenced lunch and break windows to reduce line idle time during peak shifts.
  • Deployed a real-time error-logging system with the help of team, enabling operators to flag stoppages instantly and feed the dashboard.
  • Resulted in greater uptime stability, accelerated issue resolution, and more predictable throughput.

Control

  • Embedded the automated OEE dashboard into the daily production management routine and trained shift leads to leverage insights during stand-up huddles.
  • Standardized SOPs for data capture and issue escalation to ensure consistency across shifts.
  • Achieved 2-operator redeployment with zero output loss, sustaining the OEE trajectory toward the 83% target..
