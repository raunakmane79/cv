01 • Problem identification

Losses were concentrated in stoppages, speed loss, and reporting delay.

The assessment focused on the main causes reducing availability and performance.

01
Recurring minor stoppages

Short stops reduced availability and were not logged consistently.

02
Manual KPI preparation

Reports took time to compile and delayed daily review.

03
Break-window overlap

Break timing created avoidable idle time during peak demand.

Initial condition

  • Stops were reviewed after they affected flow.
  • Downtime patterns were not visible quickly.
  • Manual KPI preparation delayed shift review.

Recommended operating state

  • Daily huddles use one KPI view.
  • Fault-code logging supports faster triage.
  • Break windows are sequenced around peak demand.
02 • Diagnostic analysis

DMAIC linked losses to measurable controls.

The project moved from problem scoping to data validation, root-cause analysis, improvement design, and control.

Define

  • Scoped a 90-day diagnostic with Production and Maintenance.
  • Targeted minor stops, speed loss, utilization gaps, and OEE.

Measure

  • Analyzed 300+ PLC and line-report records.
  • Built Excel and Power BI dashboards for OEE, downtime, and speed loss.

Analyze

  • Used Pareto, 5-Whys, and FMEA to prioritize losses.
  • Linked losses to maintenance response, break overlap, and missing logs.

Improve

  • Recommended dedicated support for conveyor and robotic-arm stops.
  • Implemented fault logging and revised break-window timing.

Control

  • Integrated the dashboard into daily shift huddles.
  • Standardized data capture, error tagging, and escalation.
03 • Solution design

Controls for uptime, response time, and flow.

Recommendations were designed for shop-floor use and daily review.

BI

Digital KPI Enablement

  • Automated OEE, downtime, and speed-loss reporting.
  • Created one KPI source for daily huddles.
SW

Shift & Break Optimization

  • Adjusted break timing around peak production windows.
  • Reduced overlap between staffing gaps and line demand.
MT

Rapid Response Maintenance

  • Focused maintenance support on recurring minor stops.
  • Used fault-code tags for faster triage.
ER

Real-Time Error Logging

  • Captured fault codes at operator level.
  • Fed weekly Pareto action review.
LM

7 Muda Reduction

  • Identified motion and re-handling waste.
  • Recommended visual controls and clearer flow cues.
LB

Line Balancing

  • Balanced workload across line stations.
  • Supported ~2 FTE redeployment with output maintained.
