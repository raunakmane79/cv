03 / Supply Chain Planning

The inventory framework defined what to stock, when to order, and how to reduce stockout risk.

The framework classified parts by cost impact, customization risk, supplier lead time, usage pattern, and production criticality.

A
Custom / High Risk

Order per Customer Order

Used for high-cost, bulky, customized, or configuration-specific components.

B
Standard / Controlled

Min-Max Stocking

Used for repeat-use components requiring controlled replenishment before stockouts occur.

C
Low Cost / Frequent

Bulk Stocking

Used for low-cost, frequently used small parts where holding risk is low.

Selected Policy

Order per Customer Order

Used for expensive, bulky, or customer-specific parts. This avoids holding inventory that may not match the final build requirement.
